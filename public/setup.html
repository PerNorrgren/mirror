Accept payments through this app
If you don't want to charge for membership or courses at all, switch this off — every pricing and checkout screen disappears, and you manage access manually from the admin panel instead.
If you keep this on, you'll still need to add your own Stripe keys as environment variables in Railway (STRIPE_SECRET_KEY, STRIPE_WEBHOOK_SECRET) — those are secrets and can't be entered here. Payments will simply show "not yet available" until you do.
